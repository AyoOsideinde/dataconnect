10.00am to 11.00am Mapping the data initiatives in the UK government

'Data' means many different things in government, and there are many organisations with different roles and responsibilities undertaking lots of data-related initiatives. This makes it difficult to keep track of who is doing what. Over the summer, the Open Data Institute (ODI) has been crowdsourcing a list of the UK government organisations responsible for data and the projects they're involved in. In this session, the ODI discuss what they found with key data leaders in government.
