11:00am to 12:30pm Keynote: ICO and Cabinet Office: Trusted access and use of government data

Public trust is essential in allowing unencumbered government data sharing. In this session we will explore the role of trusted access and appropriate use of data to deliver positive outcomes to the public and in communicating the benefits of data sharing in ways that resonate.

Our experienced panel will discuss the merits of ensuring appropriate messaging and data use in furthering their policy and delivery agendas, as well as how to tackle negative public perceptions of government data use and mistrust.

Format: Presentations and roundtable
Speakers: Viv Adams, Principal Policy Adviser - ICO
Dr Murat Soncul, GDS on 'How data protection legislation permits you to share data';
Phillipa Nazari, Assistant Director IG and Data Protection Officer, Greater Manchester Combined Authority and Transport for Greater Manchester.
Sam Roberts, CDDO on 'Data and public trust: transparency and engagement for meaningful digital transformation'.
Dr Patrick Kiernan, CDDO on the Digital Economy Act
Dr. Louise Sheridan, Deputy Director for Strategy at the Centre for Data Ethics and Innovation
