1:00pm to 2:00pm Data Governance & Management Opportunities in Transition / Change Environments

Description: A session to discuss risk mitigation and opportunity strategies, for improved governance and management of data, in a change environment.

The session will allow open discussion around the following topics...

  • Causes of data decay and gaps in change.
  • Data Management Maturity/Capability Assessments DMMA .
  • Organisational readiness & change.
  • Governance Operating Models / new Infrastructure.
  • Data Ownership and Stewarding.
  • Opportunities, in data documentation, cataloging, standardisation, normalisation, joining, tagging and registers in new ecosystems.
  • Cleansing strategy.
  • Migration standards framework.
  • RDM / MDM.

Speaker: Graham Steer, a Data Architect and Governance Consultant in MOJ HMPPS.
