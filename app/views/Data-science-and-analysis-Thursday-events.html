12:30pm to 1:00pm How to identify vulnerable citizens before and during an emergency

An emergency event in a neighbouring authority highlighted how hard it was to identify vulnerable persons during a gas leak. This highlighted the requirement to find a faster, more accurate and secure method for quickly identifying those in need during any type of emergency incident where local residents might be in danger. This talk will cover how this has successfully been achieved with impressive results. .
