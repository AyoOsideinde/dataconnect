DataConnect 21 is a week-long event organised by the Data Standards Authority and the Government Data Quality Hub, and running from 27 September to 1 October 2021. It will be a celebration of all things data across government and the public sector.

Join us for:

  • speakers from across government, the wider public sector and commercial organisations talking about the challenges and opportunities connected data brings
  • in person events, including a hackathon for APIs and data science in Manchester
  • a practical stream with experts sharing skills
