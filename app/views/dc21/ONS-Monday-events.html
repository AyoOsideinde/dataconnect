11.30am to 12:30pm Keynote from Alison Pritchard - Data on a roll in the Public Sector

Description: Join Alison Pritchard, Deputy National Statistician and Director General for Data Capability at the Office for National Statistics (ONS) as she gives her keynote speech on the first day of DataConnect21.

Come along to hear Alison talk about how we can achieve a brighter data future in the public sector, overcome challenges and build new opportunities.

Bio: Alison became Deputy National Statistician and Director General for Data Capability at the Office for National Statistics (ONS) in October 2020.

She was Interim Director General of the Government Digital Service (GDS) from August 2019 to September 2020.

Alison was previously Director of EU Exit and Transformation at GDS. Before joining GDS in 2018, Alison was Director of Transformation at the Department for the Environment, Food and Rural Affairs.

She began her Civil Service career in 1987. She has worked across a range of departments including the Ministry of Defence, HM Treasury, Cabinet Office, Home Office and the Department for Culture, Media and Sport.
