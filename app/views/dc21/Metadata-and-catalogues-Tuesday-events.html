2:00pm to 3:00pm Metadata - the backbone of the data value chain

An online presentation on metadata for UK government and public sector people. Mandy Beasley, Senior Data Architect at the Office for National Statistics, will talk about why metadata is the backbone of the data value chain, and the importance of this back-bone to your data management and business processes.
She'll also talk about how ONS are evolving the back-bone of our data value chain and how this back-bone will support the ONS' data strategy and business transformation in the future.

We will share use cases, approach, lessons learnt and anything you ask me. (Probably)
