11:00am to 12:00pm Where to begin with quality of admin data – a framework to assess administrative data quality for statistics

Kathryn Reynolds and Ella Williams Davies from the Office for National Statistics present a prototype of a framework to help with the assessment of admin data quality when using it for statistics. The session will include a run-through of the guidance, and an interactive component where you will be invited to share your thoughts.

Format: Presentation

Speakers: Kathryn is a data specialist with experience working in the public and non-profit sectors. She is passionate about ensuring that data can deliver real impacts for real people and working towards improving data quality in government to enable this. She is currently developing frameworks and guidance to support this mission. Ella is an Assistant Methodologist in the Methodological Research hub in ONS. She has an interest in administrative data quality and methods, both qualitative and quantitative, and is working on a range of projects in this area.
