9.00am to 10.00am BEIS’s Data Management Service, with Karl Hoods, CDIO BEIS

Join Karl Hoods as he leads a session on the challenges of delivering a modern Data Management Platform that will enable BEIS to manage the full end-to-end lifecycle of data.

Bio: Karl is an experienced Chief Information Officer (CIO) and has worked in the private, public and third sectors. He is currently Chief Digital and Information Officer at the Department for Business, Energy and Industrial Strategy (BEIS) where he is responsible for the delivery of core services, digital transformation, innovation, knowledge management and shared services. In addition he is SRO for the cross government technology interoperability agenda.
Prior to joining BEIS Karl was CIO for Save the Children where he was responsible for the delivery of technology and digital services to support humanitarian, policy, advocacy and fundraising activities. He has also held business technology leadership roles with web and sports media startups and led major change programmes for publishing leaders TSO. His career in startups fostered his interest in how emerging technology can be applied to business problems.
